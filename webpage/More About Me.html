Personality Test



First off, I completely concur that this test is valid from my point of view. It indicated an introverted result of 86%; knowing myself, I would position myself in the 80–100% range. Not all introversion is inherited. At an early age, it is affected by your surroundings, and our genes give us some latitude in how we respond.

Secondly, the nature section's grade is wholly unreliable. My interpersonal abilities are considered a rare asset based on my personality. I think self-happiness always comes first. You might use a lot of energy trying to please others, but when you're at your lowest, no one is there to lend a helping hand. That's why the test questions were geared toward doing things for other people rather than for yourself. I believe the remaining score can be considered accurate because it is what I would have predicted for myself before taking the personality test.
